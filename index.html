Frequently Asked Questions

How does Monkey Block work?

Monkey Block tracks your time on distracting websites and shows a visual timer. When you reach your daily limit, it blocks the site. You can also use Monkey Shield to hide specific elements.

Is it really free?

Yes! The core features are free to use. We're planning premium features for power users in the future, but essential blocking and timer functionality will always remain free.

Can I customize which sites to block?

Absolutely! You can add any website to your blacklist, set individual time limits for each site, or use combined limits for all sites together.

Does it work on all websites?

Monkey Block comes pre-configured for major social media sites and you can add any custom website to your blacklist.
