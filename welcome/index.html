Quick Setup Guide

1

Pin the Extension

Click the puzzle icon in your browser toolbar and pin MonkeyBlock for easy access. You'll use it often!

2

Set Your Time Limits

We've pre-configured 30-minute limits for popular social media sites. Adjust them to match your goals in the Settings tab.

3

Enable Social Cleaner

Turn on Social Cleaner to remove distracting elements like shorts, comments, and recommendations from social media.

4

Try Impulse Check

Enable the Impulse Check feature for a mindful pause before accessing distracting sites. It really helps!
